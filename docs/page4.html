Known issues

  • Fiosa is not replying to me
    If Fiosa is not replying to your messages, it could be because of multiple reasons. Most likely, the server handling the AI has overloaded, and you need to wait a few minutes for it to recover. It could also be because of an incorrect or deactivated OpenAI key. You can change your OpenAI key by clicking the settings or cog icon in the app.
  • It is taking forever for Fiosa to reply!
    When this happens, it is likely due to high usage of OpenAI services. All that can be done is to wait for the services to become more stable, or for OpenAI to fix the issue.
  • Fiosa is generating text from the human/user's perspective!
    This happens sometimes due to glitches in the AI. Fiosa will give itself prompts for no apparent reason. These are usually isolated incidents, and don't happen very often.
  • Experiencing an issue not on this list? Contact us below!
