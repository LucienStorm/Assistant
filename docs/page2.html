Getting your OpenAI Key

This "key" or "token" allows you to access the AI services that power Fiosa. Here's how to do it.

First of all, make an OpenAI account. To do this, please follow this link:

Click here

This will take you to the login page. Click on "Sign up" and login with Google, Microsoft or Email. Once you do this, it will ask you for your phone number. This is required to verify you are a real person and not a robot.


Once you're logged in, you should see a dashboard interface with colorful buttons. Click on the 'Personal' button as shown below.

Now click on 'View API keys' in the dropdown menu. Then click 'Create new secret key' as shown below. 

Now click "Create" and copy the long piece of text that is shown. Paste that into the dialog asking you for the OpenAI token, and click on "Submit."
